01 — The Problem

Market research is broken.

The industry is a $80B market built on three failures that have only worsened with AI.

i
Bot contamination
SurveyMonkey, Pollfish, and Prolific cannot reliably distinguish humans from AI. Reports estimate 30-60% of survey responses on commodity platforms are now bot or AI-generated.
ii
Worker exploitation
Mechanical Turk pays $1-3/hour. Prolific takes 33% from researchers. Respondents see pennies for work that resells for hundreds of dollars per response.
iii
No verifiability
Corps pay for "verified panels" with no on-chain proof. Demographics are self-reported and unverifiable. Quality scoring is opaque.
The quality of consumer research data has collapsed precisely as the cost of generating fake responses has dropped to zero.

Premium research firms (Nielsen, Ipsos, Kantar) charge $50-300 per response with weeks of turnaround. They sit at the top of a market that has no middle. Below them: commodity panels of unverifiable humans and AI. There is no infrastructure for fast, verifiable, fairly-paid human research.

02 — Legaicy's Approach

Verified humans, paid fairly, on chain.

A protocol that solves all three failures with a single architecture: subscription-gated humans, AI-scored quality, optional Proof of Personhood, on-chain settlement, and 80% revenue to contractors.

For users

Subscribe with 0.005 ETH/year. Answer questions to build your trust ranking. Stake LGCY to unlock higher-paying tiers. Earn ETH when corps buy your tier's data, plus LGCY tokens for every quality answer. Verify with Worldcoin or Humanity Protocol to unlock Trust 7+ and access premium queries up to $1,500 per response.

For corporations

Buy access to verified human research at any scale. Pulse polls (50 responses, $150-500). Premium studies (200 responses, $2,000-5,000). Authority research with PoH-verified humans (500 responses, $15,000-35,000). Filter by trust level, demographics, and verification provider.

Every answer is a piece of work. Every contributor is a verified human. Every dollar paid is auditable on chain. Every protocol decision compounds toward LGCY value through automated buy-and-burn.

04 — Trust as Infrastructure

Sybil resistance through layered verification.

Trust is the hardest problem in research data. Legaicy stacks five mechanisms — economic, behavioral, AI-based, and cryptographic — to make Sybil attacks economically irrational.

01
Subscription gate
0.005 ETH per year locks out trivial spam. A botnet of 1,000 fake accounts costs 5 ETH upfront with no guarantee of return.
02
AI quality scoring
Claude Haiku scores every answer 0-100. Below 60 quality earns zero. Generic AI-generated answers score 40-55 consistently.
03
Behavioral analysis
Writing fingerprints, response timing, mouse patterns, and tab-switching detect copy-paste from external AI tools.
04
Proof of Personhood
Trust 7+ requires verification through Worldcoin (iris scan) or Humanity Protocol (palm scan). One credential per wallet, enforced on-chain.
05
Progressive flag system
Three-strike enforcement. Flag 1: payout docked. Flag 2: cooldown extended. Flag 3: account suspended pending admin review with 30-day SLA.
06
Permanent PoH ban
Confirmed bad actors lose all staked LGCY (burned), forfeit ETH (to vault), and have their PoH credential permanently blocked.
Bots can be cheap or convincing. Not both. Legaicy makes them expensive.

08 — Protocol Vault

Four buttons. Anyone can press.

The Protocol Vault automates LP stabilization and buy-and-burn. Anyone can trigger any action; the protocol pays a 0.33% rebate to the caller. Cooldowns prevent abuse.

A
Add Liquidity
When LP is below 2% of market cap, brings LP to 3%. Pairs incoming ETH with LGCY from reserve (Phase 1) or market buys (Phase 2). 2-hour cooldown.
B
Buy & Burn
When LP is at or above 2% of market cap, buys LGCY from market within 3% slippage and sends to dead address. Permanent supply reduction. 2-hour cooldown.
C
Claim LP Rewards
Collects accumulated V3 LP fees. ETH portion returns to vault. LGCY portion enters burn queue. 24-hour cooldown.
D
Burn LGCY
Burns LGCY queued from LP fees and slashed positions. Permanent supply reduction. 24-hour cooldown.

All parameters (2% threshold, 3% target, 3% slippage, 0.33% rebate) are immutable. Cooldowns are admin-adjustable in Year 0 only and are renounced after audit. The vault has two phases: while the 4M LGCY reserve is non-zero, Add Liquidity uses reserve LGCY (Buy & Burn is disabled). Once reserve hits zero, all four buttons activate and the vault becomes fully market-driven.

09 — Trust-Weighted Governance

Earned voice, not bought voice.

Most DAOs let whales buy votes. Legaicy's governance weights voting power by trust level squared, with a logarithmic stake bonus that rewards LGCY accumulation without letting any single holder dominate.

Voting power formula

voting_power = trust_level² × stake_multiplier

Trust² creates exponential separation between trust levels. A Trust 9 user has 81× the voting power of a Trust 1 user before stake is considered. Stake multiplier is linear from 0-100K LGCY (capped at 2.0×), then logarithmic above that — approaching but never reaching 3.0×. Whales remain incentivized to accumulate, but their marginal influence diminishes sharply.
