Privacy By Design

No individual data is ever sold. Only aggregate intelligence.

No Raw Data Exposure

All corporate query results are AI-synthesized aggregate intelligence. No verbatim quotes. No wallet addresses. No individual-level data at any tier. Corporations receive market insights - not personal information.

Right to Be Forgotten

Participants can delete their answers from the corpus at any time. Soft delete removes your contribution from all future queries while preserving your LGCY and ETH earnings. Your data, your choice.

Pseudonymous By Design

No name, email, phone, or location is ever required or stored. Your wallet address is your only identifier - pseudonymous by design. Answers are associated with a cryptographic address, not a person.

Natural Market Balance

No artificial caps. Category depth and demand shape contribution flow, and contributors self-select toward under-supplied areas.

Corpus Balance Visibility

Category depth and usage are visible per segment. High corpus depth lowers per-contribution fee distribution while thin categories receive more query allocation.

Choice Rights

  • Trust 7+ can choose category
  • Trust 9 can choose category and tier
  • Everyone else receives weighted random delivery

Dashboard Output

Query fees are distributed to contributors proportionally based on contribution quality and corpus weight. No earnings are guaranteed.
